Our Mission Statement

The University of Mary Washington Alumni Database allows our faculty to easily stay in contact with and keep our alumni informed of campus activity. We want to do everything we can to take care of our Eagle Community, and especially in these times, want to keep our alumni connected to opportunities to enrich their careers and our school. We kindly ask all Mary Washington alumni to submit an alumni form so we can make UMW better together.

Eagle Crawl

The Eagle Crawl is our biggest young-alumni event of the year. Mary Washington alumni gather each March for a pub crawl through favorite downtown watering holes.
